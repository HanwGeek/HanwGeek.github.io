About me

Hi! I'm a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the FUSELab at the University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Bin Chen. I earned my M.Sc. from Peking University in 2023, advised by Prof. Zhou Huang and Prof. Yu Liu, and my B.Eng. from Wuhan University in 2020, advised by Prof. Zhipeng Gui. I also served as the Student Director of the AAG GISS-SG from 2023 to 2024 and was a software developer intern at Esri in 2020. Feel free to contact me!
